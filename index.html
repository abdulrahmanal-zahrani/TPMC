Basis of calculation. Rates reflect the SAMA maximum tariff schedule for firms, companies & individuals (banks may charge less, per the schedule's general note). This is an internal working tool — always reconcile results against the actual facility/LC agreement and the issuing bank's advice before booking or disputing a charge.

L/C Setup

Standard basis: ¼% on the first SR 400,000 for 3 months (or part), ⅛% on the excess for 3 months (or part), 1/16% per month (or part) beyond 3 months.
Extension period
The extension is charged on the same basis as the original guarantee/credit period for the remaining validity, not a separate discounted rate.
Other amendment (non‑amount)
Flat fee + actual telex/cable/mail cost — applies to amendments that do not involve an increase in amount (e.g. tenor wording, beneficiary details).
Advising an L/C carries a flat fee — no percentage commission applies.

Guarantee Setup

Standard basis: ¼% p.a. on the first SR 10,000,000, ⅛% p.a. on the excess. Fees on guarantees are to be charged on the actual validity term, pro‑rated from the annual rate — same method applies to extensions.
Note: charging separate mail/postal expenses on guarantees, and splitting guarantees into government / non‑government pricing tiers may be restricted by the guide.
